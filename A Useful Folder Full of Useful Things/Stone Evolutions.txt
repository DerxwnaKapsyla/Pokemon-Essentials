Nearly very puppet is in the HumanShape Egg Group, so you can freely
mix and match breeding for the sake of getting the perfect egg move
setup if you truly wish. The exceptions are as follows:

Kedama is in the Ditto group
Tori is in the Flying group
Genji is in the Field/Flying groups
Shanghai, Hourai, and Goliath cannot breed
Namazu is in the Field/Water 1 groups
Ruukoto and Mimi-chan cannot breed
Speed Rin is in the Field group
Zombie Fairy cannot breed
(Chibi) Kikuri is in the Chaos group
(Chibi) Yuugenmagan is in the Chaos group
Chibi Shingyoku is in the Flying/Dragon groups
(Evolved Shingyoku forms are in Humanshape, though)
